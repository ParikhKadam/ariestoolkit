Mi2 OpenSource Pc Suite For Xiaomi Mi2(s) [aries]

This is an open source Pc Suite For your Xiaomi Mi2(s)

Features:

# 1-Install Custom Rom And Recovery

# 2-Unbrick Phone

# 3-Sync Music and Photos

# 4-Backup And Restore

# 5-Unistall Apps

# 6-Edit Partitions

# 7-Shell Commands

# 8-More on the Way

What Do you Need?

#1-Linux Or Unix or Windows Pc (Mac Os X too because it's a Unix based os)

#2-Adb and Fastboot (only on unix/linux, on windows they're include yet)

#3-a Usb Cable

#4-Xiaomi Mi2 / Mi2s (Mi2A doesn't work, if you want to fork this to add its support, tell me and you're welcome)
